col_mean = []
col_var = []

# Plotting Histograms
for h in header[:-1]:
    pd.DataFrame.hist(data[[h]])
    plt.title("Histogram for " + h)
    plt.xlabel(h)
    plt.ylabel("No. of Obs")
    plt.show()
    # plt_name = os.getcwd() + "/task1/histograms/hist_" + h
    # plt.savefig(plt_name)
    col_mean.append(pd.DataFrame.mean(data[[h]])[0])
    col_var.append(pd.DataFrame.var(data[[h]])[0])

The histograms for X_i are plotted above.

Histogram of X_1 is normally distributed with fat tails.

Histogram of X_2 tells us that there are 6 distinct bins for that variable and they all occur with nearly same frequency.

Histogram of X_3 and X_5 both demonstrate a decreasing exponential behavior.

Histogram of X_4 is like X_2, with 3 distinct bins, and most values located between 0-1.
